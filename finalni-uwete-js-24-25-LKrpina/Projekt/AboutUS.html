Meet Our Team

Tome Hardy

Owner

Once a fearsome fire-breather who terrified villages, Tome Hardy now prefers the quiet crackle of parchment to the roar of flames. This reformed dragon traded his hoard of gold for shelves of books, his claws perfect for delicate page-turning rather than destruction.

Clara Chapter

Part-Time Bookseller

Abandoned as a hatchling near a smoldering library ruin, Clara Chapter was rescued by Tome Hardy—a reformed fire-breathing bibliophile who couldn’t bear to see a book (or a tiny dragon) left uncared for. Now, she’s the beloved mascot and apprentice of The Dragon’s Den, living her best life among stories and tea stains.

Walter Story

Bookfixer/Tinkerer

Walter Story, a blue dragon with a knack for tinkering, was scolded by his parents for "wasting time fixing useless things" instead of guarding treasure like a proper dragon. Exiled from his lair, he stumbled upon Tome Hardy’s bookshop, where the shelves creaked and the lanterns flickered—a place full of things needing repair. With a grin and a turn of his tail-wrench, Walter found his true hoard: a home where lonely books (and dragons) get second chances.
